iGeoTrack - Intelligent GPS tracking

iGeoTrack helps all kind of businesses and individuals track a vehicle fleet location, speed, odometer in real time, get insight and make the best decisions.

More information about iGeoTrack

Software development is our core expertise. We are dedicated to providing consulting services in marketing, design, development, and management of diverse software solutions for both corporate and governmental sectors.
